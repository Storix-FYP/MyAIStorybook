FYP Goals and Objectives
To create an AI-powered storybook
generator that empowers non-technical users
(parents, teachers) to create personalized,
illustrated, and narrated storybooks.
Core Problem: Existing tools suffer from
character inconsistency, poor narrative structure,
and require technical prompt engineering skills.

Our Solution
Character Consistency: Same character, every page.
Structured Narrative: Coherent, age-appropriate stories.
User-Friendly: Simple prompts and interactive modes.
Key Features: Chatbot co-creation, voice commands,
and styled PDF exports.

Feedback after FYP-1 defense:
Extend the scope (broader customization, better comparative analysis)
Address accuracy: Story authentication, image/narrative similarity, chatbot
consistency
Implement further checks for appropriate content

What we did
Added STT
Image–Text similarity:
CLIP (OpenAI’s ViT-B/32, or open-source ViT-L/14)
Visual similarity (character consistency):
Same CLIP model (image–image cosine similarity)
Text–text coherence:
Sentence Transformers (all-MiniLM-L6-v2, etc.)
