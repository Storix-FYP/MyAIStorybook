What is My AIStoryBook?
• AI-powered storybook generator
• Creates personalized, illustrated, and
narrated storybooks
• Supports both interactive co-creation and
simple generation modes
• Ensures character consistency across all
story pages

What Users Want?
• Parents: Want safe, educational, personalized content without
screen time concerns
• Teachers: Need customizable teaching tools with moral/cultural
lessons
• Children: Require engaging, interactive content that stimulates
imagination

Problems with Existing
Solutions
01 Character Inconsistency: Traditional AI generates
different character appearances across story pages
02 Lack of Structure: AI-generated stories often lack
proper narrative flow and age-appropriate
content
03 Limited Personalization: Difficulty incorporating
user's own characters or preferences
04 Complex User Interface: Existing tools require
prompt engineering skills
