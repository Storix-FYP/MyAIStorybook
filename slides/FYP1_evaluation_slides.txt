## 2. FYP Goals & Objectives (1 min)
- Empower non-technical users (parents, educators) to create personalized, illustrated children's storybooks quickly and privately.
- Iteration 1 goal: Complete core story pipeline—text story and optional images—from simple prompt to PDF export.
- All processing is local (privacy-first, no cloud needed).
- Leverage FastAPI backend, Next.js frontend, Ollama LLM and Stable Diffusion.
